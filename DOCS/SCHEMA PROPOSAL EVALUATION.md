---
title: SCHEMA PROPOSAL EVALUATION
category: checklist
---

- [ ] What is the **[TAG SCHEMA]()** being proposed?
- [ ] Does the proposal suggest any **[CHILD TAGS]()**?
- [ ] Is the **value** and/or **uniqeness** obvious immediately?
	- [ ] How **complex or simple** is the **greater concept** the schema communicates?
	- [ ] Even though it's being proposed as a **schema**, would it still **stand on its own as a tag**?
	- [ ] Is it mainly a **container** for other tags?
- [ ] Is it **distinct enough** from existing schemas?
	- [ ] Does it **conflict** with any existing tags or schemas? 
	- [ ] Which **existing tags** does it **compete with**?
	- [ ]  Which of the **current official tags and schemas** are **most adjacent** to **this one**? 
	- [ ] Will people likely **struggle to understand it** quickly and easily?
- [ ] How **many letters** is the tag?
	- [ ] Will creating a **shorthand version** be **fairly easy** or **more difficult**?
	- [ ] How **well** do the **first 3-4 letters** of the word **preserve enough** of the **original meaning**?
	- [ ] Have the **first 3 letters** of the tag **already been assigned** as a **shorthand** for an **existing tag**?
	- [ ] If yes, have the **first 4 letters**?
